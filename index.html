📜 Community Guidelines

These simple rules help us maintain the amazing atmosphere that makes Nothing SMP special:

🤝 Respect Everyone

Kindness and respect form the foundation of our community. Zero tolerance for harassment or toxicity.

🛡️ No Griefing

Respect others' creations. Never destroy or steal without permission.

⚖️ Play Fair

No hacks, x-ray, duping, or exploits. We value legitimate gameplay.

🏗️ Ask Before Building

Coordinate with neighbors before building nearby. Communication is key!

💬 Stay Connected

Active participation makes our community thrive. Engage with fellow players!

🎉 Have Fun!

Remember, it's a game! Collaborate, create, and enjoy every moment.
